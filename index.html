OpenTrials

All the Data, on All the Trials, Linked

Open Trials will aggregate information from a wide variety of existing sources, and aims to provide a comprehensive picture of the data and documents on all trials conducted on medicines and other treatments around the world.

Open Knowledge are working on this project with Ben Goldacre as Chief Investigator. Ben Goldacre is a Senior Clinical Research Fellow in the Centre for Evidence Based Medicine at the University of Oxford. His blog is at www.badscience.net and he is @bengoldacre on twitter.
